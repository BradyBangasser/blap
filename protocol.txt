BLAP protocol, version 0.1, developed by Brady Bangasser 9/10/24

Packet Structure
    Header:
        BLAP packets will start with the 4 bit id, which will be 1101
        That will be followed by another 4 bit seqeuence that is currently reserved for future use
        4 bytes resevered
        1 byte frag seq
        2 byte payload length 
        
    Payload:
        payload, consider encrypting and compression algorithms

    Trailer:
        none yet
